MISSION

Political Governance

  • To promote democratic consolidation through developmental governance and inclusive development
  • To strengthen the capacity of political parties towards policy evaluations
  • To measure quality of governance in Africa

Economic Governance

  • To train African trade negotiators at bilateral, regional and multilateral levels.
  • To strengthen regional economic communities.
  • To build capacity for trade and industrial policy formulation and implementation at the national level.
  • To boost the quality of macro-economic governance.
  • To foster migration and regional integration.

Mo Ibrahim Foundation

The Mo Ibrahim Foundation (MIF) is an African foundation, established in 2006 with one focus: the critical importance of governance and leadership in Africa. It is our belief that governance and leadership lie at the heart of any tangible and shared improvement in the quality of life of African citizens.
Leadership and governance in Africa
Leadership is about making choices, defining priorities, and taking risks. Governance is about properly documenting and effectively implementing these choices.
