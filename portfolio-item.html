Policy Studies and Evaluation

A missing link in the various intervention programmes in Africa is inadequate policy evaluations. Whereas government departments, regional and continental organisations as well as donor organisations execute various programmes, not much is being done in the areas of evaluating these policies so that the shortcomings can be identified.

Our intervention will include policy evaluations on key areas of policy at the national, regional and governmental levels. Expected funders will include: European Union, United States Agency for International Development (USAID), Regional Economic Communities, African Union

Staff Requirement:

  • We will have three staff per project both for sourcing funds and execution.
  • We will also be working in partnership with colleagues in the various Departments of the university

  • Prepare full budget for each of the activities
